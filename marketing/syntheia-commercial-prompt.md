## Production Notes for Syntheia

### **Visual Style & Aesthetic**
- **Color Palette:** Primary Sky Blue (#0ea5e9), Purple Accent (#8b5cf6), Gradients from blue to purple
- **Typography:** Modern, clean sans-serif (like Inter, Space Grotesk)
- **Mood:** Professional yet innovative, trustworthy yet cutting-edge
- **Reference Brands:** Salesforce, Adobe, Microsoft Dynamics, Workday

### **Music & Sound**
- **Style:** Modern corporate, inspiring, building momentum
- **Tempo:** Start slow/contemplative, build to energetic/triumphant
- **Instruments:** Synthesizers, subtle electronic elements, orchestral swells for impact moments
- **Avoid:** Generic stock music feel, overly dramatic, too "tech-bro"

### **Pacing**
- 2 minutes total runtime
- Quick cuts during problem section (urgency)
- Smooth transitions during solution reveal
- Medium pace during feature showcase (let information land)
- Testimonials: 5-6 seconds each
- Strong, memorable ending with clear CTA

### **AI Avatar/Presenter Option**
If using an AI presenter:
- Professional appearance: Business casual or business formal
- Age range: 35-50 (appeals to CPA decision-makers)
- Tone: Confident, warm, knowledgeable, not salesy
- Setting: Modern office backdrop or clean neutral background

### **Key Messaging Points to Emphasize**
1. **30-50% faster audits** - Primary value proposition
2. **AI that catches what humans miss** - Differentiator
3. **PCAOB compliant** - Critical for credibility
4. **500+ CPA firms already using** - Social proof
5. **14-day free trial, no credit card** - Low barrier to entry
6. **Human-in-the-loop** - AI assists, humans decide

### **Target Audience**
- CPA firm partners and managers
- Audit practice leaders
- CFOs evaluating audit software
- Age: 35-60
- Pain points: Time pressure, staff burnout, compliance burden, legacy software frustration
