# Board of Directors Meeting Minutes - Extract
## Aura Holdings Inc.
## Fiscal Year 2025 - Key Resolutions

---

## Q1 2025 BOARD MEETING
**Date:** March 15, 2025
**Location:** 350 Fifth Avenue, New York, NY (Virtual option available)
**Attendees:** All directors present (quorum achieved)

### Key Resolutions:

**Resolution 2025-Q1-001: Approval of 2024 Audited Financial Statements**
RESOLVED, that the Board of Directors hereby approves and adopts the audited consolidated financial statements for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2024, as presented by management and audited by [Audit Firm].
*Vote: Unanimous*

**Resolution 2025-Q1-002: Declaration of Q1 Dividend**
RESOLVED, that a quarterly dividend of $0.15 per share be declared, payable April 15, 2025, to shareholders of record as of April 1, 2025.
*Vote: Unanimous*

**Resolution 2025-Q1-003: Approval of 2025 Operating Budget**
RESOLVED, that the 2025 operating budget as presented, totaling consolidated revenues of $825,000,000 and capital expenditures of $50,000,000, be approved.
*Vote: Unanimous*

**Resolution 2025-Q1-004: CEO Compensation Approval**
RESOLVED, that the compensation package for CEO Jon Toroni, including base salary of $750,000, target bonus of 150% of base, and equity grants per the compensation committee recommendation, be approved.
*Vote: 6-0 (Mr. Toroni abstained)*

## Q2 2025 BOARD MEETING
**Date:** June 14, 2025
**Location:** Virtual Meeting
**Attendees:** All directors present (quorum achieved)

### Key Resolutions:

**Resolution 2025-Q2-001: Approval of Sub C Sydney Expansion**
RESOLVED, that the Board approves capital investment of up to $5,000,000 for the Sydney, Australia office expansion, including leasehold improvements and equipment.
*Vote: Unanimous*

**Resolution 2025-Q2-002: Declaration of Q2 Dividend**
RESOLVED, that a quarterly dividend of $0.15 per share be declared, payable July 15, 2025.
*Vote: Unanimous*

**Resolution 2025-Q2-003: Authorization of New Credit Facility**
RESOLVED, that management is authorized to negotiate a $50,000,000 revolving credit facility with JP Morgan Chase, subject to final terms approval.
*Vote: Unanimous*

## Q3 2025 BOARD MEETING
**Date:** September 13, 2025
**Location:** 350 Fifth Avenue, New York, NY
**Attendees:** All directors present (quorum achieved)

### Key Resolutions:

**Resolution 2025-Q3-001: Approval of Sub A Manufacturing Expansion**
RESOLVED, that the Board approves construction of new production facility in Texas, with budget of $25,000,000, to be funded through equipment financing and operating cash flow.
*Vote: Unanimous*

**Resolution 2025-Q3-002: Declaration of Q3 Dividend**
RESOLVED, that a quarterly dividend of $0.15 per share be declared, payable October 15, 2025.
*Vote: Unanimous*

**Resolution 2025-Q3-003: Related Party Transaction Approval**
RESOLVED, that the intercompany management fee arrangement between Parent and subsidiaries, at 3% of subsidiary revenues, be ratified as arm's length.
*Vote: Unanimous (related parties abstained)*

**Resolution 2025-Q3-004: Settlement Authorization**
RESOLVED, that management is authorized to settle the Industrial Components Corp. litigation for up to $600,000.
*Vote: Unanimous*

---

## Q4 2025 BOARD MEETING
**Date:** December 13, 2025
**Location:** Virtual Meeting
**Attendees:** All directors present (quorum achieved)

### Key Resolutions:

**Resolution 2025-Q4-001: Approval of 2026 Operating Budget**
RESOLVED, that the preliminary 2026 operating budget, projecting consolidated revenues of $950,000,000, be approved in principle, with final approval in Q1 2026.
*Vote: Unanimous*

**Resolution 2025-Q4-002: Declaration of Q4 Dividend**
RESOLVED, that a quarterly dividend of $0.15 per share be declared, payable January 15, 2026.
*Vote: Unanimous*

**Resolution 2025-Q4-003: Appointment of External Auditors**
RESOLVED, that [External Audit Firm] be retained as independent auditors for fiscal year 2025, with fees not to exceed $2,200,000.
*Vote: Unanimous*

**Resolution 2025-Q4-004: Goodwill Impairment Assessment**
RESOLVED, that the Board acknowledges management's qualitative and quantitative goodwill impairment assessment and concurs that no impairment is required.
*Vote: Unanimous*

**Resolution 2025-Q4-005: Going Concern Assessment**
RESOLVED, that the Board has evaluated the Company's ability to continue as a going concern and concludes there is no substantial doubt about the Company's ability to continue as a going concern for twelve months from the financial statement date.
*Vote: Unanimous*
