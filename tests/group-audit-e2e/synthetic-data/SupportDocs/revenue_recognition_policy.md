# Revenue Recognition Policy Memorandum
## Aura Holdings Inc. and Subsidiaries
## ASC 606 Compliance Documentation
## Fiscal Year 2025

---

## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This memorandum documents the Company's revenue recognition policies in accordance with ASC 606 - Revenue from Contracts with Customers. The Company has evaluated all revenue streams and determined the appropriate recognition methodology based on the five-step model.

---

## 2. REVENUE STREAMS BY ENTITY

### 2.1 Parent Company - Aura Holdings Inc.

| Revenue Stream | 2025 Revenue | Recognition Method |
|----------------|--------------|-------------------|
| Management Fees | $85,000,000 | Point in time - as services rendered |
| Royalties | $42,000,000 | Over time - as underlying sales occur |
| **Total** | **$127,000,000** | |

**Management Fees (ASC 606-10-25-27)**
- **Performance Obligation:** Provision of corporate management services to subsidiaries
- **Transaction Price:** 3% of subsidiary revenues (arm's length per transfer pricing study)
- **Recognition:** Monthly, as services are performed
- **Variable Consideration:** None

**Royalties (ASC 606-10-55-65)**
- **Performance Obligation:** License of intellectual property (patents, trademarks)
- **Transaction Price:** 4% of net sales using licensed IP
- **Recognition:** Sales-based royalty recognized when subsequent sale occurs
- **Constraint:** Not recognized until underlying sale is probable

### 2.2 Sub A - US Manufacturing

| Revenue Stream | 2025 Revenue | Recognition Method |
|----------------|--------------|-------------------|
| Product Sales | $285,000,000 | Point in time - upon delivery |
| Service & Maintenance | $28,500,000 | Over time - ratably over contract |
| **Total** | **$313,500,000** | |

**Product Sales (ASC 606-10-25-30)**
- **Performance Obligation:** Manufacture and deliver industrial equipment
- **Transaction Price:** Fixed contract price less volume discounts
- **Recognition:** Point in time when control transfers (FOB shipping point or destination per Incoterms)
- **Warranty:** Assurance-type warranty (1 year); accrued as cost, not separate performance obligation
- **Returns:** Less than 1% historical; estimate accrued based on history

**Service & Maintenance (ASC 606-10-25-27)**
- **Performance Obligation:** Stand-ready maintenance services
- **Transaction Price:** Fixed annual fee
- **Recognition:** Ratably over the contract period (typically 12-36 months)
- **Contract Modifications:** Prospective treatment for scope changes

### 2.3 Sub B - EU Distribution

| Revenue Stream | 2025 Revenue (EUR) | 2025 Revenue (USD) | Recognition Method |
|----------------|-------------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Product Distribution | €185,000,000 | $200,725,000 | Point in time |
| Installation Services | €18,500,000 | $20,072,500 | Over time |
| **Total** | **€203,500,000** | **$220,797,500** | |

**Product Distribution (ASC 606-10-25-30)**
- **Performance Obligation:** Distribution of manufactured products
- **Transaction Price:** Cost plus margin (standard pricing)
- **Recognition:** Point in time upon delivery per DAP/DDP Incoterms
- **Principal vs Agent:** Sub B is principal (controls goods before transfer)

**Installation Services (ASC 606-10-25-27)**
- **Performance Obligation:** Installation of equipment at customer site
- **Transaction Price:** Hourly rate or fixed fee
- **Recognition:** Over time using input method (hours incurred / total estimated hours)
- **Significant Judgment:** Estimation of total hours to complete

### 2.4 Sub C - APAC SaaS

| Revenue Stream | 2025 Revenue | Recognition Method |
|----------------|--------------|-------------------|
| SaaS Subscriptions | $125,000,000 | Over time - ratably |
| Professional Services | $22,000,000 | Over time or point in time |
| Support & Maintenance | $18,500,000 | Over time - ratably |
| **Total** | **$165,500,000** | |

**SaaS Subscriptions (ASC 606-10-55-54)**
- **Performance Obligation:** Stand-ready access to cloud platform
- **Transaction Price:** Monthly/annual subscription fee; tiered pricing
- **Recognition:** Ratably over the subscription period
- **Variable Consideration:** Usage-based fees recognized as usage occurs
- **Significant Judgment:** Determination that SaaS is a single performance obligation

**Professional Services (ASC 606-10-25-27)**
- **Performance Obligation:** Implementation, training, customization
- **Transaction Price:** Fixed fee or time & materials
- **Recognition:**
  - Implementation: Over time (customer consumes benefit as work performed)
  - Training: Point in time upon completion
  - Customization: Over time if customer-specific; point in time if generic
- **Integration with SaaS:** Evaluated for distinct performance obligations

**Support & Maintenance (ASC 606-10-25-27)**
- **Performance Obligation:** Technical support and software updates
- **Transaction Price:** Percentage of subscription or fixed fee
- **Recognition:** Ratably over the support period
- **Bundling:** Often bundled with SaaS; allocated using relative standalone selling price
